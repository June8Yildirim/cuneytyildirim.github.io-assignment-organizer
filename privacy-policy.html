Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Assignment Organizer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

User Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

User-Generated Content

  • Assignments (titles, notes, due dates, priorities)
  • Subjects/Classes (names, colors, room numbers, building information)
  • Timetables/Schedules (class times, days, locations)
  • User preferences (theme settings, notification preferences)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your data across your iOS devices via iCloud
  • To send you local notifications for assignment reminders
  • To personalize your experience within the app

Data Storage and Synchronization

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using CoreData. This includes assignments, subjects, timetables, and user account information.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud in your device settings, your data will be synchronized across all your iOS devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This synchronization is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

You can disable iCloud sync at any time through your device's Settings app. When disabled, your data remains stored locally on each device.

Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptographic methods
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • All data transmission to iCloud is encrypted using Apple's secure protocols
  • We do not have access to your password or authentication tokens

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

  • iCloud/CloudKit: Used for data synchronization across devices. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Keychain Services: Used for secure credential storage.
  • WidgetKit: Used for home screen widgets.
  • UserNotifications: Used for local notification delivery.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with Apple's iCloud service if you choose to enable iCloud sync.

Analytics and Tracking

Assignment Organizer does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. We do not collect usage statistics or track your behavior.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual assignments, subjects, or your entire account at any time through the app
  • Export: Your data is stored locally and can be accessed through your device
  • Correction: You can edit or update your information at any time

Children's Privacy

Assignment Organizer is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
